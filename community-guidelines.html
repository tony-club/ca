Community Guidelines

Last updated: 2025-08-22

1. Be Respectful

Treat all players and community members with respect. Harassment, hate speech, and bullying will not be tolerated.

2. Play Fair

Do not use cheats, exploits, or any third-party software to gain an unfair advantage. Keep the competition fun and fair for everyone.

3. Keep it Clean

Avoid sharing inappropriate content, spam, or malicious links. Help us maintain a safe and welcoming environment.
