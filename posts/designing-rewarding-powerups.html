Designing Rewarding Power-ups

Why shields, slow-mo, and multipliers keep players returning.

Published 2025-08-22

A temporary shield lets players experiment without losing progress, raising engagement and skill. Brief slow-motion during spikes helps players regain control and feel powerful. Score multipliers tied to consecutive success reward mastery and create thrilling risk/reward loops. These simple mechanics transform a good game into an addictive one by giving players moments of power and a clear path to higher scores.
